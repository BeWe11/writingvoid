Why does this site exist?

Sometimes my fear of the blank page prevents me from writing anything at all.

Freewriting, the act of writing anything that comes to your mind for a few minutes, can help overcoming this fear by forcing you to write without judging what you've written.

I've found that I cannot stop judging the quality of what I've written during freewriting even if I try. The simple fact that the result of my writing lies before me on the screen triggers me look for typos, grammar mistakes and bad style.

This site was created to help me practice freewriting. The text field deletes everything that was written in it after two seconds, preventing me from judging my output altogether.

I could just use a text editor and delete my output manually every few seconds. I've found that knowing that what I write will be deleted automatically helps keeping anxiety low.

I've also written a small site that contains writing exercises. There's not a lot of content on it, but it's free and might help you to ignite your creative spark.

If you have any feedback or suggestions for this site, write me an email.
